## TL;DR

- **The Atto 3 is the entry SKU** for ASEAN dealers building a Chinese-EV import business in 2026 — proven sales, real service network, accessible price point.
- **Used 2022-2024 units** clear Sihanoukville landed at $14,500-19,500 USD depending on trim and mileage. Vietnam Hai Phong adds ~$800-1,200 to the same unit.
- **Customer fit**: urban daily-drive buyers in Phnom Penh, Hanoi, HCMC, Vientiane. Range and charging infrastructure are no longer the bottleneck in these capitals.
- **Battery is the price-defining check**: a State-of-Health (SoH) ≥88% retains resale; ≤80% drops 25-35% in trade-in value.
- **Margin math at $17,500 landed**: target $22,500-24,500 dealer resale in Phnom Penh, $24,000-26,500 in Hanoi for clean 2023 units.

## Why the Atto 3 specifically

Among Chinese EVs being imported into ASEAN in 2026, the Atto 3 is the brand-and-segment crossover that works for the most dealer customers. It is not the cheapest (Wuling Bingo is). It is not the most prestige (BYD Han is). It is the **mid-market BEV with the cleanest supply curve, the broadest service-network coverage, and the most-recognized brand badge** outside of Toyota and Tesla in our target markets.

This translates to:

- **Faster turnover** than less-known Chinese EV SKUs (HVH Aion, Neta, Leapmotor).
- **Tighter trade-in pricing** because the model has a public reference benchmark.
- **Lower buyer education burden** — most prospective buyers have seen Atto 3 on the road in their city by 2026.

If you are starting a Chinese-EV import business, this is the first SKU to stock. If you already have an established Toyota/Honda business and want to add an EV anchor, this is the first SKU to add.

## The vehicle: spec quick reference

The Atto 3 is a compact SUV / crossover EV built on BYD's e-Platform 3.0, in production since early 2022 for China and exported globally since mid-2022.

| Spec | 2022-2024 used | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Body | 5-door SUV (4,455 mm length) | Comparable to Toyota Corolla Cross / Honda HR-V |
| Powertrain | Single front motor, 150 kW | 1-spd reduction |
| Battery | 50.1 / 60.5 kWh LFP "Blade" | LFP cells = 3,000+ cycle life |
| Range (NEDC) | 410 / 510 km | Real-world ~325/400 km mixed |
| DC fast charge | 88 kW | 0-80% in ~50 minutes |
| AC charge | 7 kW | 0-100% in ~9.5 hours |
| Drive | FWD only | No AWD variant for this generation |
| Cargo | 440 L (seats up) | Acceptable for daily-drive use |

The Atto 3 was never a performance car. It was BYD's bet that mid-market buyers wanted a quiet, comfortable, well-equipped EV at $25-30K new. That positioning translates well to year-3 used imports.

## The landed cost math (Sihanoukville example)

Worked example: 2023 BYD Atto 3 Boost (50.1 kWh, 510 km), 28,000 km, condition B+, sourced from Suzhou, shipped to Sihanoukville.

| Cost line | USD |
|---|---|
| Dealer purchase (China, used) | $13,800 |
| UCarsea margin + inspection + docs | $1,400 |
| **FOB Shenzhen (our quote)** | **$15,200** |
| Sea freight Shenzhen → Sihanoukville | $850 |
| Cambodia CIF | $16,050 |
| Import duty (35% × CIF for cars) | $5,617 |
| Special tax (15% × CIF×1.35 for EV — preferential rate) | $3,250 |
| VAT (10% on CIF×1.35×1.15) | $2,490 |
| Clearance + inland to Phnom Penh | $580 |
| **Landed Phnom Penh** | **~$27,987** |

The Cambodian EV preferential rate (15% special tax vs ~30-45% for ICE comparables) is what makes this math work. Same vehicle as a 2.0L gasoline SUV would land closer to $34-38K.

See the full duty mechanics in our [Cambodia Used Car Import Duty 2026](https://blog.ucarsea.com/cambodia-used-car-import-duty-2026/) breakdown.

A 2023 Boost trim with 25-30K km landing at $27,000 in Phnom Penh and clearing dealer retail at $23,500 leaves margin of roughly **-$3,500 below cost** on that single unit. The math breaks at that landed cost.

**What actually works**: source the same vehicle for $13,000-13,500 FOB (lower-mileage 2022 Active trim, or sharper purchasing through volume), keeping landed Phnom Penh around $24,500-25,500, and resale around $23K-25K. Margin then runs $0-2K plus you build a dealer relationship for repeat business.

The honest read: **single-unit margin is thin on Atto 3 right now.** This is a volume-and-relationship trade, not a high-margin trade. Dealers who buy 5-10 Atto 3 per quarter from a stable supplier earn through repeat ordering, not through hero margins on individual units.

## The battery health check (the single most important inspection step)

A used Atto 3's resale value tracks two numbers: **mileage** and **battery State of Health (SoH)**. Mileage you can see on the dashboard. SoH requires either:

- BYD diagnostic tool (dealer-only access in China)
- Aftermarket OBD2 + AppCar / BYDLink mobile app reading
- Empirical range test under controlled conditions

Acceptable SoH thresholds for export inventory:

| SoH range | Status | Resale impact |
|---|---|---|
| ≥ 92% | Like-new | Premium pricing, no concession |
| 88-91% | Good | Standard pricing |
| 82-87% | Fair | -10 to -15% concession to buyer |
| 75-81% | Marginal | -20 to -30% concession, disclose to buyer |
| < 75% | Avoid | Do not import — battery replacement risk |

LFP cells (Blade battery) hold up well — most 2022-2023 Atto 3 units we inspect run SoH 88-94% even at 50-80K km. The outlier risks are:

- Vehicles used as ride-share / fleet (high cycle count)
- Vehicles stored long-term in hot climates (less common in Eastern China sourcing)
- Vehicles with prior accident damage to the pack (visible in CarFax-equivalent records)

We refuse to FOB-quote Atto 3 units with SoH below 82% — the dealer ends up explaining it to a frustrated buyer at year 2 and the relationship sours. It is not worth $300 of saved purchase cost.

## Customer profile that converts on Atto 3

Don't sell the Atto 3 to everyone. The right buyers in 2026:

- **Urban daily commuter** in Phnom Penh, Hanoi, HCMC, Vientiane — 30-50 km/day, home charging available.
- **Tech-aware professional** under 45 — comfortable with EV operation, infotainment expectations match what Atto 3 delivers.
- **Replacement buyer** for a Camry / CR-V / Vios that is at 200K+ km — running cost differential pays for itself in 18 months at typical city driving rates.

Don't sell to:

- **Long-distance / inter-province buyers** — 350 km real-world range hits a wall on highway use.
- **First-time car buyers in tier-2/3 cities** without charging infrastructure.
- **Buyers who want a Camry-equivalent prestige signal** — the Atto 3 is read as "smart practical buyer," not "successful executive."

Calibrating which dealer customers you push Atto 3 to (vs Han / Camry / Geely Coolray) matters more than any other sales decision.

## Service and parts depth at year 3

BYD opened its first Phnom Penh authorized service center in 2024 and its first Vientiane center in 2025. Vietnam has had Hanoi and HCMC coverage since 2023. The Atto 3-specific service depth:

- **Brake and suspension**: standard wear items, available in 1-3 days through BYD authorized network.
- **HV battery service**: dealer-only, sometimes 1-2 week parts wait for cell-level work.
- **Infotainment unit**: replacement units available, but software updates lag China-domestic by 3-6 months.
- **Drive unit (motor + inverter)**: well-supported under BYD's 8-year/150,000 km HV warranty (warranty does not transfer to second buyer on a private re-import — disclose this).
